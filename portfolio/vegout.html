What is vegout?

After my first three weeks at DevMountain, we were tasked with developing a FULL CRUD application utilizing an API of our choice. After researching a number of different option, I decided to create an app that gave vegans and vegetarians a way to find, save, and rate their favorite recipes

This was a great project for me to utilize my new skills as web developer. It challenged me to work quickly, as we had only a weekend to develop this project. I was challenged to learn on the fly, to read and understand API documentation, and to manage my time effectively.

At the end of the weekend, I had a fairly feature-rich product that met the four basic types of functionality: to create, read, update and delete. I learned a lot through this experience: effective branching in React, important design elements, and more.
